# Launch Checklist

## Pre-launch
- Verify env vars in production
- Run smoke test
- Confirm transactional email domain health
- Seed 3 public demo packs

## Day 0
- Send onboarding email to beta users:
  - Subject: "You're in! Start your first RankPack"
  - Body: "Create a pack in 2 minutes, invite friends, and reveal the group's final ranking."

## Feedback survey template
1. How easy was pack creation?
2. Did ranking feel smooth on mobile?
3. Would you share results publicly?
4. What blocked you from completing reveal?
