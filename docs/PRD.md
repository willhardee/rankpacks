# RankPacks PRD

## Problem
Groups struggle to pick favorites quickly and share outcomes.

## v1 goals
- Create packs in <2 minutes
- Invite group by link or email
- Submit rankings in mobile-friendly flow
- Reveal consensus with insight cards users want to share

## Personas
- Host: creates pack and invites friends
- Member: ranks and comments
- Admin: moderates reports and manages beta access

## Scope
Included: pack lifecycle, ranking, results, comments/reactions/reports, notifications, admin, PWA shell.
